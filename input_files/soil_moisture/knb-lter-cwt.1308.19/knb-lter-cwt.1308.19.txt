Dataset Abstract

	Long-term soil moisture stations were established within the Coweeta Hydrologic Lab Basin to collect measurements of soil moisture, soil temperature, air temperature, and relative humidity at a range of spatial scales, from ridge to cove and low to high elevations in the southern Appalachians, and across the regional range of rainfall amounts.
